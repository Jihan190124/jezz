MORE THAN A TRAINER

Hi, I'm Alex Ryder — a certified fitness trainer and registered dietician based in Mumbai. With over 8 years of hands-on experience, I've helped 500+ clients across age groups achieve life-changing transformations through science-backed training and personalized nutrition plans.

My philosophy is simple: sustainable results over quick fixes. Whether your goal is fat loss, muscle gain, athletic performance, or simply a healthier life — I design programs that fit your body, your schedule, and your lifestyle.
